Registration

DSEI 2026 is co-located with IEEE MASS 2026. Workshop attendees register through the main conference; please follow the registration instructions, fees, and deadlines published on the IEEE MASS 2026 website.

For each accepted workshop paper to be included in the IEEE MASS 2026 proceedings and the IEEE Xplore Digital Library, at least one author is typically required to register for the conference and present the work at the workshop, in line with IEEE MASS 2026 policies. Substitute presenters may need prior approval from the program chairs; details will be stated on the main conference site.

Registration portal and fee schedules will be linked from the IEEE MASS 2026 homepage when they become available.
