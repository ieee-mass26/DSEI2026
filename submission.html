Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished work that is not under review elsewhere. All submissions must be written in English and prepared as PDF files using the IEEE Conference Proceedings manuscript templates, with a maximum length of six single-spaced, double-column pages in 10pt fonts on 8.5 × 11 inch paper, including all figures, tables, and references. Reviewing is single-blind, so author names may appear on the submitted manuscript. Submissions should be uploaded through EDAS; the link to the IEEE MASS 2026 workshop track will be announced on the main conference site. Accepted workshop papers are intended for inclusion in the conference proceedings and the IEEE Xplore Digital Library, subject to IEEE policies, presentation at the workshop, and IEEE’s right to exclude a paper from distribution after the conference if it is not presented.
