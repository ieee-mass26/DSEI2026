Paper Format

  • Submissions must be written in English and submitted as PDF files.
  • Papers should use the IEEE Conference Proceedings manuscript templates.
  • The maximum length is 6 single-spaced, double-column pages using 10pt fonts on 8.5 × 11 inch paper, including all figures, tables, and references.

Review and Publication

  • Submitted work must be original, unpublished, and not under review elsewhere.
  • Reviewing is single-blind, so author names may appear on the submitted manuscript.
  • Accepted workshop papers will appear in the IEEE MASS 2026 conference proceedings published by IEEE, subject to IEEE policies and presentation at the workshop.

Authors can also review the IEEE MASS 2026 workshop submission guidelines for conference-wide instructions.
